# Jobi - Job Posting Website

## Overview

**Jobi** is a cutting-edge job posting platform designed to streamline the hiring process for employers and simplify the job search for candidates. Whether you're an employer looking to quickly fill vacancies or a job seeker searching for your next career move, Jobi provides the tools and features you need to succeed.

## Features

- **User-Friendly Interface:** Navigate through Jobi with ease, whether you're posting a job or searching for one.
- **Advanced Search Filters:** Find the perfect job or candidate with our customizable search options.
- **Personalized Job Recommendations:** Receive job suggestions that match your skills and preferences.
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Stay updated on job postings, applications, and interview requests.
- **Application Tracking:** Employers can track applicants through every stage of the hiring process.
- **Detailed Analytics:** Gain insights into job posting performance and applicant trends to make informed decisions.

## Getting Started

1. **For Employers:**

   - Sign up and create a company profile.
   - Post job openings with detailed descriptions and requirements.
   - Review and manage applications through your dashboard.

2. **For Job Seekers:**
   - Sign up and create a personalized job seeker profile.
   - Browse job listings using advanced filters and receive recommendations.
   - Apply for jobs directly through Jobi and track your application status.
